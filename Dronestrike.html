DroneStrike

Dronestrike is a personal project created using Josh Begley's publicly availableDronestre.amAPI. It crudely simulates the view and actions of a United States drone pilot, in an attempt to provide a public narrative to the targeted killings that often receive no more than a press release. The long term effects on both the pilots and the communities effected by these strikes is largely unknonwn. Users navigate through the website using their mouse and keyboard, and all available information of the most recent strike are dynamically rendered rendered via the API. Potential trigger warning, contains graphic content.
